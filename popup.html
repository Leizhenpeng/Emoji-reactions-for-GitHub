Code review emoji guide

  • 👍 - Positive highlight.
  • 🔧 - Suggested change/refactor.
  • ❓ - Question needing response.
  • 🤔💭 - Thinking out loud/exploring alternatives.
  • 🌱 - Planting a seed for future consideration.
  • 📝 - Explanatory note or commentary.
  • ⛏ - Nitpick, often stylistic or formatting.
  • ♻️ - Suggestion for refactoring.
  • 🏕 - Opportunity for improvement.
  • 📌 - Out of scope concern for follow-up.
  • 💡 - Insightful idea or suggestion.
